SR Sensory Gym waiver summary.

This is a legal document affecting your rights. The form includes assumption of risk, parent supervision, release of liability, medical authorization, health and safety, optional photo/video consent, and facility rule acknowledgments.

  • SR Sensory Gym is a parent-supervised environment.
  • Children must be actively supervised while inside the facility.
  • All children must have a signed waiver before participation.
  • Photo/video consent is optional and not required for participation.
