Helpful details

Common family questions.

Tap any question to open the answer. Contact the team directly if your family needs additional support before visiting.

01 Is there parking available? +

Yes. A private parking lot is located behind the building for your convenience.

02 Can you wear shoes in the play space? +

Shoes are not allowed in the play space. Socks are required, and grippy socks are available for children and adults.

03 How much does Open Play cost? +

Open Play is $25 per child and $22.50 for siblings attending at the same time. The first adult is included, and each additional adult is $5.

04 When are parties offered? +

Parties are available every weekend in three time slots: 10 AM-12 PM, 1-3 PM, or 4-6 PM. For weekday parties, email info@srchildrenslounge.com.

05 What ages can attend? +

The sensory gym is designed for babies through age 9 and welcomes both neurotypical and neurodivergent children.
